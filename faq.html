    Frequently Asked Questions

  • Booking tickets online is easy! Simply visit our website, select the movie you want to watch, choose your preferred showtime, select the number of tickets, and proceed to checkout. You can pay securely using various payment methods available on our platform.

  • Yes, absolutely! Our website allows you to select your preferred seats from the available seating options. You can view the seating layout for each theater and choose seats that suit your preference.

  • We accept a variety of payment methods, including credit/debit cards, online banking, and mobile wallets. Rest assured, our payment gateway is secure and ensures the safety of your transactions.

  • Unfortunately, we do not offer cancellations or modifications for online ticket bookings once the transaction is completed. We recommend double-checking your booking details before confirming your purchase.

  • While creating an account is not mandatory, we highly recommend it for a seamless booking experience. By creating an account, you can save your preferences, view your booking history, and receive exclusive offers and updates.

  • After successfully booking your tickets online, you will receive a confirmation email containing your booking details and a QR code or booking reference number. Simply present this at the theater's ticket counter or use the self-service kiosks to collect your tickets before the show.

  • We recommend arriving at the theater at least 15-20 minutes before the showtime to ensure a smooth experience. In case of late arrival, you may miss the beginning of the movie. However, our staff will assist you in finding suitable seating without causing disruption to other patrons.

  • We strive to keep our ticket prices transparent and affordable. However, a small convenience fee may be applied to cover processing and service charges associated with online bookings. This fee will be clearly displayed before you confirm your purchase.

  • Yes, we often have special promotions and discounts for group bookings, special screenings, and events. Keep an eye on our website or subscribe to our newsletter to stay updated on the latest offers and deals.
